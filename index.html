Prog Rock Database Project

Welcome to my (George Chadderdon's) Prog Rock Database Project. This was my first effort (in 2010) to develop a database web application. It is a searchable database of progressive rock music with links to information about the bands and their albums. The phenomenon of prog rock rock began in the mid-60s in the United Kingdom, probably inspired largely by the experimentations of the Beatles. One of the earliest prog rock albums was The Moody Blues' Days of Future Passed, a concept album with orchestral interludes between the folk/world music-flavored rock pieces. The prog rock movement largely died in the mid-70s, but had a brief resurgence in the 80s. Most, though not all, of these groups are or were British. Typical prog rock groups: King Crimson, Yes, Genesis, ELP (Emerson, Lake, and Palmer), Van Der Graaf Generator.
